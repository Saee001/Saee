SECTION A: PERCENTAGES

1. A number is increased by 30% and then decreased by 30%. Net change?

Decrease of 9%
Increase of 9%
0%
Decrease of 6%
Explanation: Let number = 100 → 130 → 91. Net decrease = 9%.

2. Production falls by 20%. Increase required?

25%
20%
30%
40%
Explanation: After 20% fall, production = 80. To reach 100, increase = 20/80 = 25%.
SECTION B: DIRECTION SENSE

3. Walk 10 m north, then 10 m south. Distance from start?

0 m
10 m
20 m
5 m
Explanation: He returns to the starting point. Distance = 0.

4. Walks 4 km north, 3 km east, 4 km south. Distance?

3 km
4 km
5 km
7 km
Explanation: North–South cancels. Net eastward movement = 3 km.
